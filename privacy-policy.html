Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2026  |  Last updated: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how iSwift Solutions ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the BankRecon mobile application and related services. By using BankRecon, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you register and use BankRecon:

Account & Organization Information
  • Full name and email address
  • Organization name and details
  • Password (stored in encrypted form)
  • Subscription plan and billing history
Financial Data (entered by you)
  • Check records and transaction details
  • Cash flow entries and bank account information
  • Payee information and categories
  • Financial reports and reconciliation data
Usage & Device Data
  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • IP address and general location (country/region)
  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Crash reports and performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the BankRecon service
  • Process your subscription payments via PayMongo
  • Send account-related notifications and service updates
  • Respond to support requests and inquiries
  • Detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized access
  • Comply with legal obligations under Philippine law
  • Analyze usage to improve app performance and features

3. Advertising (FREE Plan Users)

Users on the FREE plan may be shown advertisements served by third-party advertising networks:

  • Google AdMob — for mobile app advertisements
  • Google AdSense — for web platform advertisements

These services may collect device identifiers, cookies, and usage data to deliver and personalize advertisements. This data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy and is separate from the data we collect to operate the Service.

Users on the STARTER and PROFESSIONAL plans are not shown advertisements.

You may opt out of personalized ads at any time through your device's advertising settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization).

4. Third-Party Services

BankRecon integrates with the following third-party services:

  • PayMongo — payment processing for subscription plans. We do not store your payment credentials. All transactions are handled securely by PayMongo. View their Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob / AdSense — advertising on the FREE plan. View Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure cloud servers. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and keep your login credentials confidential.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and financial data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Financial transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years to comply with applicable tax and accounting regulations under Philippine law.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format
  • Objection — object to how we process your data in certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@iswiftdev.com.

8. Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your BankRecon account and all associated data at any time by:

  • Emailing us at support@iswiftdev.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"
  • Including your registered email address in the request

We will process your deletion request within 30 days. Note that certain financial records may be retained as required by law (see Section 6).

9. Children's Privacy

BankRecon is intended for users who are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at support@iswiftdev.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you via email or an in-app notification at least 14 days before the changes take effect. Continued use of BankRecon after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the Republic of the Philippines, including the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) and its implementing rules and regulations.
